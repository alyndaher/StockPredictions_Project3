Data

S&P 500 and Consumer Price Index data was downloaded from https://datahub.io/core/s-and-p-500#readme as a csv file. This data consisted of 31 years from 1987-2017. Additionally, disaster data was extracted from https://public.emdat.be/data between the years of 1987-2017. Both datasets were limited to this time frame to allow comparable datapoints for the machine learning or multiple linear regression portion of our project.
